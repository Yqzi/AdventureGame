Alternative: In-App Deletion

You can also delete your account directly inside the app. This process is fully automated.

  1. Open the Questborne app.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap Delete Account & Data.

What data is deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and irreversibly destroyed immediately:

  • Your user authentication profile (Email address and User ID)
  • Your player save file (Stats, Inventory, Gold, Levels)
  • Your active and completed quests
  • Your game session history and AI chat logs
  • Your subscription tier record

For more information on how we handle user data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
